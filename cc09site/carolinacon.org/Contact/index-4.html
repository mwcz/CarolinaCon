CarolinaCon 2009 is over

Posted by Nick Fury on Friday April 17 at 11:14 AM


CarolinaCon 2009 has ended.  It was a HUGE success.  We would like to thank all of our attendees for making it a success.  We especially want to thank all of the speakers who generously gave their time and energy for presenting.  You guys ROCK!!!  As always, if you had fun, then tell your friends about us and bring them along for the next CarolinaCon.

If you’d like to help with the next CarolinaCon or would like to present then head over to http://forum.nc2600.org and post a comment in the CarolinaCon section and as always, stay tuned to this space for more information about the next CarolinaCon.  Get ready, it will be here before you know it!

Pre-Registration

Posted by Nick Fury on Tuesday January 13 at 05:12 PM
